Duke Basketball

I grew into my Duke basketball fandom during the Jon Scheyer 2010 Championship run. During my undergraduate education at Duke, I was an avid participant and supporter of the Duke Blue Devils. In my first year, I tented for the Duke vs. UNC game and subsequently became a Line Monitor. During my senior year, I facilitated the Duke tenting procedure for Coach K's final season as co VP of Tenting. I was fortunate to be able to author trivia test and facilitate many student events to support the Team.
