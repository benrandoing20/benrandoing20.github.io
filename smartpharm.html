Project Introduction

In recent years, the concept of a circular economy has emerged as a groundbreaking approach to address the challenges of resource depletion and environmental degradation.Janssen Pharmaceutical Companies of Johnson & Johnson (J&J) has expressed its strong commitment to achieving significant progress in climate action by enhancing the environmental sustainability of its products, operations, and supply chain. The result of a nine-month effort to conduct need finding, prototype, and devlop and professional product is OTOO. OTOO is a new model of over-the-counter medicine vending to facilitate circularity in pharmaceutical packaging streams.

Contributions & Outcomes

I acted as a founding member of this startup team to develop the OTOO product. In addition to my product management role, I was responsible for developing the UI, UX,and electronics for the cutting, dispensing, and pill counting. The final product was a fully functional vending machine that may dispense a single dose of medication on demand from a bulk cartridge of medication. The cartridge and medication packaging may be fully recycled and reused.
